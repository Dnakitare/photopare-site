Frequently asked questions

Is it safe to delete photos?
Yes. Deleted photos go to Recently Deleted in the Photos app and are recoverable for 30 days. PhotoPare never permanently deletes anything.
Does PhotoPare upload my photos?
No. All scanning and analysis happens entirely on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.
What's included for free?
Scanning your entire library and reviewing results is completely free. The one-time $4.99 unlock enables deletion.
Can I restore my purchase on a new device?
Yes. Tap "Restore previous purchase" on the unlock screen. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID.
What photo formats are supported?
HEIC, JPEG, PNG, and other formats supported by iOS. HEIC photos are decoded natively using Apple's ImageIO framework.
