Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Your photos stay on your device

PhotoPare processes all photos entirely on your device. No photos, image data, or metadata are collected, transmitted, or stored outside your device.

Photos library access

The app accesses your Photos library solely to scan for duplicates, blurry images, and screenshots. This access requires your explicit permission and can be revoked at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos.

In-app purchases

PhotoPare uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. PhotoPare does not collect or store any payment information.

No tracking

PhotoPare does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact support@photopare.app.
